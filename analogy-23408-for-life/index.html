Analogy #23408 for Life

Oct 23 | Comments

You can either be a:

  • Player - do things, live life

  • Cheering Fan - encourage others to do it

  • Disgruntled/Non-Participating Fan - hater

  • Commentator - sit on the sidelines content to watch others do well and fail

Put me in, Coach.
